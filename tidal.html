Tidal Energy

p

There are two protocols in evaluating the available energy of a tidal pool. The first protocol is based on a simple tidal pool where the discharge through the generator is rapid.

This is an ideal scenario for maximum power generation where the energy from a wave is discharged at a low-tide datum (bottom of pool).

The second protocol is more advanced. This protocol takes into account varied tides throughout the day.

This is protocol takes into account the modulated flow and therefore, provides more stable energy to the grid.
